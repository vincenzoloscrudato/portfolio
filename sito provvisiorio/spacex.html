SpaceX App onboarding concept

Role UX Research
UX Design
UI Design
Team Vincenzo Lo Scrudato
Company Personal Project

Challenge
Design the app on-boarding of the spacex travel booking app

Qualitative Interwiew:
Sample of questions:

  • What sensations did you feel in your last trip (have to be a long trip)
  • What do you think, when you watch news related to space travel?
  • Elon Musk has declared that travel to Mars will be possible on 2025: what do you thin about it?

How to make perceive the trip like a fundamental step for all humanity?

How to make the travel more “accessible” to all?

Define the strategy
Onboarding wizard
The on boarding will not be an explanation of app functionality but will be a explanation of the relationship between human and Mars: the fundamental step that humanity has faced to earn this incredible goal.
To achieve this, a “stories-based approach” will be used, as the stories are a tool used by millions of people to describe their lives: but this time it will be humanity that describes the experience.

How improve the conversion rate of the signup funnel ensuring at the same time the possibility of access to the app?

  • Friendly Tone of Voice
  • User can close the signup in every moment
